From lower primary to higher secondary, the Kerala government provides free education in Malayalam and English. Schools also provide free food to students in addition to education. Kerala is known for providing free education from primary to secondary school, as well as a much better educational atmosphere. School dropouts are caused by a variety of circumstances, including financial concerns and family troubles. Our goal is to encourage the child to continue his or her studies by assisting him or her in overcoming financial barriers. We provide school-office stationery, bags, uniforms, and travel expenses to help them overcome these limitations. We aid the family financially in extreme cases. During the school year, we provide these products or make a financial donation to our recipients.

Medical expenses are rising in today's world, and we occasionally come across folks who require financial aid to manage their medical difficulties. We encourage our members and friends to make direct deposits into the individual's bank account.

Natural disasters and epidemics could wreak havoc and incur financial burdens on our societies. We're responsible for assisting local governments, state governments, and the central government in overcoming this situation. We encourage our members to donate directly to the various government-managed disaster funds. We also pay visits to the camps, distributing essential products or donating them to government-approved collecting centres.

Thank you very much for helping us out by arranging materials, assisting with transportation, or raising funds. These activities assist those in need in overcoming adversities and resuming their lives.
